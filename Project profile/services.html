MSME Services

EXPORT PROMOTION: MSME DI provides export related information to existing and prospective exporters. It organizes training programmes on Export Management, Export Packaging, Export Marketing, Export Policies and Procedures, etc. It facilitates Micro & Small entreprises in participating in International Trade Fairs organized in various countries every year. MSME DI also nominates Micro and Small Enterprises for availing assistance under Marketing Development Assistance of NMCP Scheme.

MARKETING SUPPORT: Marketing is primarily an entrepreneurial function, all possible efforts have been made to help MSME sector in their marketing endeavour. The following services are offered under this category of assistance. 

  1. Ancillary Development: MSME DI endeavors to promote ancillary development by liaison with Large/Medium/Public Sector units(PSUs). It organizes Vendor Development Programmes and Exhibitions/Trade Fair, wherein products required by PSUs and large Industries, which can be manufactured by the Micro & Small-scale sector are identified and exhibited. This information is disseminated to other small scale and large-scale units as a part of the marketing assistance.
  2. Buyer Seller Meet (BSM)
  • To bring Micro, Small & Medium Enterprises (MSMEs), large industrial houses, Govt. Depts., PSUs and others on a common platform for business promotion.
  • To provide marketing opportunities for MSMEs.
  • To enable large industrial houses to identify competitive vendors.
  • To showcase the strength and competitiveness of MSMEs.
  • To provide an insight into modern technological developments.
  • To showcase the strength and benefits of working in industrial clusters/consortia.
  1. Government Stores Purchase Programme: MSME DI, Ahmedabad assesses the technical capacity of enterprises and assist NSIC to register micro and small enterprises under Single Point Registration scheme for participation in Government Purchases.

CAPACITY ASSESSMENT: MSME units who utilize scarce raw materials for their products need to be periodically assessed for their production capacity and requirement of raw materials. MSME - Development Institute, Ahmedabad undertakes such assessment and recommends their capacity to the competent authorities for release of scarce raw material. Under the policy measures for promoting & strengthening MSME/Tiny units, it has been enunciated that adequate and equitable distribution of indigenous and imported raw materials would be ensured to MSME sector. Based on the needs, the units are assessed & given priority in such allocation of raw materials.
 
TECHNO MANAGERIAL CONSULTANCY: The need for providing specialised service to MSME sector is increasing with the rapid development of these industries in the state. Apart from new products and items introduced, new raw materials have also come into existence, throwing open a vast field of operation & exploitation. MSMEs are, however, handicapped by lack of competent technical & management personnel as well as financial resources. In order to help MSMEs in such areas, MSME - Development Institute, Ahmedabad is offering technical and managerial consultancy services through field visits to the needy units. The officers visit the units to guide them in the following areas : 
(a) Requirement of plant & machinery including layout
(b) Use of improved mfg. Technology/design/process
(c) Improvement in tooling
(d) Product development
(e) Consultancy for diversification
(f) Management Capability improvement
(g) Offering Management training
(h) Improvement in marketing
(i) Availability of finance
(j) Other registration/policies of Govt./support services by other agencies etc. 
The above services are also rendered at MSME - Development Institute premises and through correspondence to the new entrepreneurs as well. The new entrepreneurs desirous to set up S units are guided to select their product, location, raw materials, machinery, technology & other problems, apart from the above listed areas.
 
INDUSTRIAL POTENTIAL SURVEY REPORTS: The Institute guides prospective entrepreneurs in selection of products and location. It periodically prepares State Industrial Potentiality Survey Report & District Industrial Potentiality Survey Reports of all the districts of Gujarat.

PREPARATION OF TECHNICAL REPORTS: MSME - Development Institute, Ahmedabad is well equipped by technical manpower in the fields of Mechanical, Chemical, Glass & Ceramics and Metallurgy etc. As per action plan, the technical officers prepares various technical reports such as  detail project report, project profile, directories etc. for the guidance of units and entrepreneurs

APPRAISAL OF PROJECT REPORT: The project reports prepared by the small scale units and new entrepreneurs need to be suitably verified by competent technical persons, so as to judge their technical feasibility as also commercial viability. To assist the entrepreneurs, MSEs and other Govt./Semi-Govt. agencies, project reports are evaluated and appraised at MSME - Development Institute, Ahmedabad.

DATA  COLLECTION: Economic Investigation & Statistics (EI & S) division of MSME - Development Institute, Ahmedabad functions as a nucleus for statistical information relating to SSIs. It collects, compiles & disseminates statistical information on various aspects of SSIs for planning & formulation of various developmental programmes. The main work involvement under this head includes : 2% sample selected on the basis of industrial census, primary data collection for computation of Index of industrial production for MSEs.
